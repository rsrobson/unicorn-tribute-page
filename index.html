Unicorns at a Glance

The unicorn is one of our world's most beloved magical creatures. Real or not, the unicorn has proven itself to be an unstoppable force. The hallmark horn, the flowing mane, and the ever-evolving list of magical abilities have inspired wonder and creativity in both children and adults for millennia.

Unicorns and unicorn-like creatures can be found in the histories of many cultures. The unicorn is the national animal of Scotland. The Chinese qilin may have one or two horns. Other East Asian cultures have creatures similar to the chimerical qilin. Some translations of The Bible have several references to unicorns, though other versions translate the word as "wild ox".
